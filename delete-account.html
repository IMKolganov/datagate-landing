Account Deletion

Last updated: December 2025

This page explains how to request deletion of your DataGate account.

How to delete your account

To request deletion of your DataGate account, please send an email from the Google account used to sign in to the application to:

imkolganov@gmail.com

Email subject: Delete my DataGate account

What data will be deleted

Once your request is received, the following data associated with your account will be permanently deleted:

  • Authentication identifiers
  • Account and user identifiers
  • VPN configuration data
  • Server-side user records

Deletion timeframe

Account deletion will be completed within 30 days from the date the request is received.

Data retention

Some data may be retained if required by applicable laws or regulations. Such data will not be used for any other purposes.
